== Description ==

**WordClever AI Content Writer** by WP Radiant is a revolutionary plugin designed to simplify and elevate the way you create product content for WooCommerce. With its simple registration and login process, WordClever empowers users to create professional-grade content with just a few clicks. The free version offers up to **10 content generation requests**, providing a risk-free way to explore the plugin's potential. Whether you’re launching new products or optimizing existing ones, WordClever is your go-to tool for efficient and effective content creation.

== Installation ==

**1. Prerequisites:**

Ensure that WooCommerce is installed and activated on your WordPress site before proceeding.

**2. Installing the Plugin:**

o Log in to your WordPress admin dashboard.
o Navigate to **Plugins > Add New.**
o Search for **WordClever AI Content Writer** in the plugin repository search bar.
o Click **Install Now** and then **Activate** the plugin once the installation is complete.

**3. Register and Log In:**

o After activation, go to the WooCommerce product edit page.
o Locate the **WordClever Box** on the right-hand sidebar.
o Click the **Register** button to create an account or log in if you already have one.

**4. Start Generating Content:**

o Select the desired content type (e.g., Product Description, Meta Title).
o Enter a keyword to guide the AI and choose the tone for your content.
o Set the number of results (up to 3 per request).
o Click **Generate Content** and view the AI-generated results directly in the WordClever Box.

**5. Free Version Limitations:**

o You can generate up to **10 content requests** in the free version. Upgrade to the premium version for unlimited content generation (coming soon).
